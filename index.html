Riff Raff are a fun and energetic 4 piece covers band with a combined 60+ years of experience playing live music. Our repetoire spans the decades from the 60's, including classic and modern rock hits, a twist of 80's pop, a sprinkling of 90's grunge and pop punk, some millenial indie and much more. We provide a professional and friendly service at every gig whether it be a private booking or a public event.

The Event

We have played many different events in the past including weddings, birthday parties, private corporate functions, biker rallies and local festivals. We welcome enquiries whatever the event. We are dedicated and hard-working musicians and will even learn requests for your event at an additional charge if required.

Our Repetoire

We have a repetoire spanning the decades and cover a range of genres from pop and funk to Rock and heavy metal and everything in between. We cover the party classics but can also adapt different styles for specific events. We can mix it up or tailor the set to suit the show Young and old are sure to be entertained!

Sound

We have a full PA rig which can be used at a range of venues, from small intimate function rooms to outdoor events. Our musicians have quality equiptment, enabling us to produce a great sound. We can also provide a disco for a small fee if required, whether that be for before, or after the live show and even inbetween.

Lights

Lights, camera, action! With a full professional lighting rig, we can ensure that the venue of your event has the appropriate lighting, whether thats for our live shows or for the required disco. We will bring lights to suit the show unless requested not to.
